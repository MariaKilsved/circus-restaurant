Our Services

Toilets: They are located on left near the entrance. They are equipped with nappy changing tables, breast feeding room and disabled customer toilets.

Storage Lockers: They are located on right near the entrance. Available to use free of chanrge. After 10.00 pm every day the lockers are emptied and what is left in the lockers is handed in to Lost & Found near Entry door.

Strollers : They can be parked near entrance in the designated area..

Parking for visitors : On the main street, there are paid parking spaces available for visitors. Standard commun parking rates applicable.

Accessibility for Wheelchairs : Entry to restaurant is accessible with Wheelchair. Ramps are available on request
